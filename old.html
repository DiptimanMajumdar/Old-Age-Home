WHY OLD AGE HOME?

Parents play an important role in our lives. They brought up their children despite of having socio-economic difficulties. They fulfill our every demand & never complain for anything. In return its our duty to give respect, love & care to them.

IDEA/CONCEPT

Parents who took care of us our whole life no matter what the situation they faced and children when they grow up they are sending their parents to old age home just because they think that its below them or its such a headache. We need to remove this bridge, this gap needs to be brought closer and should be held for generations.

What can be done?

  1. We can meet with people who considered sending their parents to old age home and convince them.
  2. We can take help from NGOs.
  3. We can do social awareness.
